Universal Taro Indonesia

“Out of sight, out of mind”

Universal Taro Indonesia focuses on taro agricultural products and their derivatives. We provide the best quality in Indonesia to meet the taro needs desired by consumers, we can provide the best quality and price to consumers.

We have a mission to introduce Indonesian taro, especially taro Pratama to the world, because taro Pratama is the original tuber or fruit that we develop in Indonesia, we are committed to providing the best quality to investors or consumers.

We also work with several farmer groups in Indonesia to provide counseling and understanding on how to sort and care for farmers to provide quality tubers.
